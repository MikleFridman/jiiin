Welcome to Jiiin!

Jiiin is a free online assistant for accounting and recording clients. What can he do?
At the moment, we have:
  • Branches (service addresses)
  • Services (cost, duration, linked to branches)
  • Staff
  • Opening hours (for branches and employees)
  • Clients (personal data, session history, tags)
  • Calls (reminder to contact the client on the right day)
  • Accounting for income and expenses
  • Payment receipts (link for client)
  • Restricted access for employees
  • Downloading data to Excel
  • Multilingual interface (russian, english)
  • ...
Register, use it, and give us feedback. Together we will make the best product!
